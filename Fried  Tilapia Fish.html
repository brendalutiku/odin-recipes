Fried Tilapia Fish Description

Fried Tilapia Fishis fish from the lake that has been prepared through deep frying in cooking oil.In my country we refer this meal for people from the lakeluos.Fried Tilapia Fish is an extremely mouthwatering,delicious and mild spiced recipe which is known as a traditional Pakistani recipe.Fish fry is an easiest starter recipe that can be prepared with less efforts due its fast and easy process in preparation.Fried Tilapia Fish are a good source of omega3 fatty acids that helps in the reduction risk of heart attack and stroke.It is also rich in calcium and phosphorus and a great source of minerals,such as iron,zinc,iodine,magnesium and potassium.It is recommended we eat fish atleast twice a week as part of healthy diet.

Ingredients For Tilapia Fish Preparation

  • A full fresh tilapia fish from the lake
  • cooking oil
  • herbs
  • fish marsala

Steps in Preparation

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Wash the fish and slice it from the sides
  3. Apply the fish marsala plus the herbs on the fish
  4. Leave the fish to absorb the herbs and the marsala
  5. Place your frying pan on heat
  6. Put the cooking oil on the pan leave the cooking oil to heat properly
  7. Place the fish inside the frying pan with cooking oil leave it to fry
  8. Serve it with Ugali or any other preferred meal
