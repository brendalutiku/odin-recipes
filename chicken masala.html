Ingredients

  • 750 grams chicken
  • 4 tablespoon mustard oil
  • 2 cup blended onions
  • 1/2 cup blended tomatoes
  • 0 as required salt
  • A bit of red chillipowder,coriander ,tumeric,blackpepper and dry ginger.

Steps In Preparation

  1. Gather your ingredients
  2. Saute your onions and tomatoes wash and blend them
  3. Wash your Chicken and chop in required shapes
  4. Place your frying pan on a gas cooker
  5. On the frying pan add the mustard oil leave it to heat
  6. Add the blended onions allow it to cook before adding garlic
  7. Add the herbs followed by tomatoes leave it to cook
  8. Add the chopped chicken use a cooking spoon to mix well
  9. Leave it to cook for some minutes then add the cream
  10. Leave it cook then serve with whatever you want to serve with
