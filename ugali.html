Ugali Recipe

A Describtion on Ugali

Ugali is a staple food for most of the bantu speaking groups in my country.Over the years ugali has become known and consumed by almost everyone in my country.Ugali is prepared by adding maize flour in cooked water.It is loved by many due to is ability to provide energy to our body parts and believed once consumed we would have energy.It is one of the simplest recipes.

Ingredient

  • maize flour
  • water

Steps on preparation

  1. boil the water until it produces bubbles
  2. add little flour until it becomes firm
  3. Serve with other preffered meals
